About Us

Are you deathly scared of earthquakes but also an avid traveler? Are you sick of wasting time checking multiple sites to cross reference seismic information with your travel plans? Here at Movers Not Shakers© we strive to simplify your query needs by condensing both into one easy to use search engine. Simply enter the City, State or Country in which you plan to travel and you will be provided with the most recent seismic activity along with the weather for that area.
